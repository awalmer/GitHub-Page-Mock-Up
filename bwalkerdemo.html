Demo of HSS by Professor Bruce Walker

YouTube Video

Description

This is a live tour and demo of the Highcharts Sonification Studio (HSS) by Professor Bruce Walker of Georgia Tech. The demo covers the HSS landing page, Data and Charts tabs, visual and audio settings, how to import and export, and more. Recorded as part of a demonstration in the Sonification World Chat, in May 2023. You may want to play this back at 1.5x or 2.0x speed.
